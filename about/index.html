About

The Cooperative

Fork & Shoe is a performance production company that focuses on interactive, accessible work, community-building puppet shows, and brass band music, all across the Boston area. We operate based on values discussed and agreed to by the Co-op. Read these values here.

The Goddamn Brass Band

The Goddamn Brass Band is a Boston area contemporary ensemble that provides musical accompaniment for many of Fork & Shoe's outings.
